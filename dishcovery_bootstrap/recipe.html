Roasted Veggie Power Bowl

30 min · Easy · Serves 2

High fiber Meal prep friendly Gluten-free option

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, carrots, bell peppers)
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges, to serve

Overview

Roast colorful vegetables on a single pan, then layer them over fluffy quinoa with a bright, lemony dressing. Perfect for quick weeknights or make-ahead lunches.
